Bloodblitz

This is our game Bloodblitz. It is a multiolayer game where you must kill the other player in order to win. Player 1 uses the arrows to move and N to attack. Player two uses WASD to move. The two different charachters are the Warlock and the knight. The Warlock can stab people while the knight uses a big sword to kill. Each player gets to pick one charachter but can pick the same one.
